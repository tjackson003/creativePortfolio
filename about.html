about me.


My name is Terrel Jackson, but I go by TJ. I'm a content creator in the Northwest Indiana and Chicago area, who likes to make content that hopefully makes an entertaining and/or inspiring dent in the world. I majored in digital video and audio production and emerging media at Ball State University and graduated in 2016. People always ask me what "emerging media" is, and I laugh and say, "That new app or whatever you haven't heard about yet, but it's gonna be real big one day."

I am a continuous learner. Learning for me equates to exploring new ideas and growing as an individual. I express my creativity and imagination by either making things or outright physical expression. But, at the moment, I stratch my itch via my comedy-based YouTube, "Coolyville", sit and talk podcast, "A Pretty Neat Podcast", Sundays spent in improv classes at Chicago's Second City Training Center, and freelance video work and graphic design.
